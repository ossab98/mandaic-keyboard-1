mandaic keyboard how to use?

how to use?

First, you must download and install the mandaic font on your computer.

⚠️ Your computer will not recongize the Mandaic alphabet without installing the font. You will see a ▢ instead of letters.

how to install your font on mac OS?

  • Download the mandaic font
  • Double-click the download file to unzip it
  • Open the font book app
  • Click on the User tab on the left
  • Click on the + icon besides the User title
  • In the Finder menu, navigate to your unzipped file, then click Open
